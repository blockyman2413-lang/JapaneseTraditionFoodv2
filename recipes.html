Sushi Ramen Tempura Mochi Soba Takoyaki

Sushi Recipe

Cook Japanese rice, mix with rice vinegar, sugar and salt. Add sliced fish, cucumber or egg. Roll with seaweed or shape by hand.

Ramen Recipe

Boil noodles, prepare broth with soy sauce or miso, add egg, spring onion, meat or vegetables. Serve hot.

Tempura Recipe

Dip shrimp or vegetables into cold flour batter. Deep fry until crispy and golden. Serve with dipping sauce.

Mochi Recipe

Steam glutinous rice or use mochi flour. Pound or mix until sticky. Shape into small balls and fill with sweet red bean paste.

Soba Recipe

Boil buckwheat noodles, rinse with cold water, and serve with dipping sauce. Add spring onion and seaweed.

Takoyaki Recipe

Make batter with flour, egg and dashi. Add octopus pieces into round molds. Cook until round, then top with sauce and bonito flakes.
